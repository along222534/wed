Types of Computer Viruses

Learn about different types of computer viruses, their behavior, and examples:

  • Trojan Horse: Malicious software disguised as legitimate.
  • Worm: Self-replicating virus that spreads through networks.
  • Ransomware: Encrypts your data and demands payment for decryption.
  • Spyware: Software that secretly gathers user data.
  • Adware: Displays unwanted advertisements.
  • Rootkit: Hides malicious activities from detection.
  • File Infector: Embeds itself into files and spreads.

Prevention Tips

Follow these tips to protect your system:

  • Install reputable antivirus software.
  • Keep your operating system and applications updated.
  • Avoid clicking on suspicious links or downloading unknown files.
